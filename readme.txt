== Description ==

Use Whipclip to find and embed the best moments on TV, live, as they happen.  Whipclip is the only 100% legal way to search, clip, and embed TV.

The Whipclip Plugin for WordPress lets you explore and embed the best TV moments, as they happen, as you work. Our plugin features high quality, 100% legal, TV clips from your favorite shows.  The clips are so easy to embed that no coding knowledge is required. Whipclip videos are available directly in the editing page within the WordPress CMS.

Features of the Whipclip Plugin for WordPress:

Explore trending TV moments 
Search TV clips by keyword
Filter by TV show
Add clips to any page or post
Adjust video size and alignment
Enable Autoplay video

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'Whipclip Embed' to `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or install directly through the ‘Plugins' menu in your WordPress Admin
1. Activate the plugin through the “Plugins” menu in WordPress.
1. When on the post page, click the “Whipclip Embed” button and choose your embed.
